WELCOME TO THE TRADEMARKS APPLICATION SYSTEM (TAS)

TRADEMARKS FILING GUIDELINES

YOU NEED THE FOLLOWING TO REGISTER A SUCCESSFUL TRADEMARK ONLINE

  1. A DULY ACCREDITED AGENT ACCOUNT
  2. A VALID ONLINE GENERATED TRANSACTION ID
  3. APPLICANT INFORMATION(NAME, ADDRESS, PHONE NUMBER AND EMAIL)
  4. TRADEMARK INFORMATION WHICH INCLUDES A SCANNED IMAGE OF THE DEVICE IN JPEG FORMAT
  5. POWER OF ATTORNEY( NOT COMPULSORY AT THE FILING STAGE BUT MUST BE SUBMITTED BEFORE AN ACCEPTANCE LETTER CAN BE ISSUED)

HOW TO FILE MY APPLICATION AFTER I HAVE MADE PAYMENT

  1. LOGIN WITH YOUR USERNAME AND PASSWORD
  2. ON THE AGENT DASHBOARD MENU, CLICK ‘FILE ONLINE APPLICATION’
  3. SELECT THE PAYMENT OPTION USED TO GENERATE THE VALID TRANSACTION ID AND CLICK TO OPEN
  4. LOCATE THE TRANSACTION IN THE TABLE OF APPROVED TRANSACTIONS DISPLAYED AND CLICK ‘FILE TRADEMARK’ TO BEGIN
  5. READ THE INSTRUCTIONS CAREFULLY AND CLICK PROCEED TO FILE
  6. ON THE FORM 1 DISPLAYED, ENTER THE APPLICANTS INFORMATION (NAME, ADDRESS, EMAIL, PHONE NUMBER) AND CLICK CONFIRM DETAILS (CONFIRM DETAILS ENABLES YOU DO CROSS-CHECK ON WHAT YOU HAVE ENTERED INTO THE FORM AND MAKE CORRECTIONS IF ANY BEFORE YOU CONTINUE TO THE NEXT STAGE)
  7. CLICK CONTINUE TO GO TO FORM 2
  8. SELECT THE LOCAL OR FOREIGN OPTION (IF THE APPLICATION IS FOREIGN, SELECT FOREIGN AND IF NIGERIAN, SELECT LOCAL)
  9. SELECT THE TRADEMARK TYPE (WORD, WORD AND DEVICE, DEVICE. IT IS IMPORTANT TO SPECIFY THE TYPE TO ENABLE THE UPLOAD BUTTON FOR THE BROMIDE IF APPLICABLE
  10. ENTER THE TRADEMARK TO BE REGISTERED ( FOR TRADEMARKS WITH DEVICE, WE ADVICE YOU ADD DEVICE AFTER THE WORD E.G TAS & DEVICE)
  11. ENTER THE CLASS (1-45) AND THE SPECIFICATION OF GOODS TO BE REGISTERED AS YOU WANT THEM TO APPEAR ON THE ACCEPTANCE AND CERTIFICATES.
  12. INDICATE IF YOU WISH TO CLAIM OR DISCLAIM ANY PART OF THE TRADEMARK
  13. CLICK CONFIRM DETAILS TO UPLOAD BROMIDE AND DOCUMENTS IF APPLICABLE AND CLICK CONTINUE TO FORM 3
  14. ENTER THE ADDRESS OF SERVICE INFORMATION (PLEASE NOTE THAT THE ADDRESS OF SERVICE MUST BE A NIGERIAN ADDRESS.)
  15. CLICK CONFIRM DETAILS AND CONTINUE TO FORM 4
  16. PROVIDE THE ATTORNEY INFORMATION. PROCEED TO CLICK CONFIRM DETAILS TO SUBMIT FORM.
  17. AFTER PROVIDING THE REQUIRED INFORMATION, AN ACKNOWLEDGMENT FORM WILL BE ISSUED TO YOU.

HINT:

  • YOUR ACKNOWLEDGEMENT HAS ALL INFORMATION SUBMITTED TO THE REGISTRY INCLUDING YOU ONLINE APPLICATION ID (OAI) WHICH ENABLES YOU TRACK THE STATUS OF YOUR APPLICATION USING THE ‘CHECK STATUS’ BUTTON ON THE MENU BAR.
  • YOU ACCEPTANCE WILL BE ISSUED TO YOU AFTER YOUR TRADEMARK HAS GONE THROUGH A SUCCESSFUL EXAMINATION PROCESS.
