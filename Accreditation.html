WHAT IS ACCREDITATION?

Accreditation is a mandatory process for law firms, stakeholders and potential registrants that wish to act as agents. Accreditation helps to identify a legal practitioner or agent in the area of Industrial Property Registrations

BENEFITS OF BEING ACCREDITED

1.    Access to the Online filing platform
2.    E-payments with Debit Cards
3.    Application Status check
4.    Access to fees and Forms
5.    Secure filing procedures

REQUIREMENTS FOR ACCREDITATION

  1. Bio Data of Principal Partner (First Name, Surname, Nationality, Date Of Birth, State Of Origin)
  2. Company information (Name, Address, Email, Phone Number, Contact Person, Contact Number, Date Of Incorporation)
  3. Account Login Information ( Preferred Username And Password)
  4. Supporting documents (CAC Certificate, Letter Of Introduction Of Company And Its Representatives, Passport Photograph Of Principal Partner)
  5. A one-off fee of N23, 500  for Corporate institutions and N13, 500  for individuals


HOW TO APPLY

You can apply online simply by following the steps below

  1. Click the get accredited link under the secure member section
  2. Read the Instructions and select preferred institution (individual or Corporate)
  3. Complete the form displayed and attach supporting documents
  4. Take note of your username and password as you would be required to login after submitting your form
  5. Click submit and you will receive an automatic email with all the information provided when completing the accreditation
  6. Login into your account under the secure member section and click complete accreditation in your user area to generate a successful invoice.
  7. Print out invoice and proceed to www.quickteller.com/tpdr to make payment with your debit card or any Skye bank in Nigeria.  

AFTER YOU APPLY

All applications will be reviewed by the Accreditation Manager. If the Review is successful, you will receive an email confirming your approval. Automatically, your online services links will become active.

AFTER ACCREDITION

When your account is activated you will be able to do the following
1.    Make payment for services using your debit MasterCard
2.    Pay for more than one item using PayExpressSolution
3.    Fill online Application form (form 2), attach your bromide and power of attorney (form 1)
4.    Print your acknowledgment online immediately.
5.    Publication of your accepted marks are automatic and FREE
6.    Track your application online using the Status Check module
7.    Access to other services online (Application for Certificate, Mergers, search etc)
